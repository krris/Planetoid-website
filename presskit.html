Description

Planetoid is a minimal, graph-based strategy game with ambient music. Each level is built from planets and each of them grows over time. Your goal is to capture all enemy planets by shooting bullets. Take note that while aiming the gameplay is slowed down. It gives you more time to think about your strategy and choose an ideal power of the bullet.

Key features

  • Mind bending
  • Tough AI
  • Graph-based strategy
  • Slow motion
  • Many difficult levels
